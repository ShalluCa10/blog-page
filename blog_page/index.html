My Youtube Content Creator Journey

Hello! I'm Shallu Sameera, a passionate YouTube content creator. I am from Punjab, India. I am an extrovert personality. A girl with too many goals. I love to travel and record amazing things in my phone and post in my Youtube Channel I love sharing my knowledge and experiences through engaging videos. My channel focuses on tech reviews, tutorials, and canada lifestyle mini vlogs. Join me on this exciting journey!

I would love to share my journey through this blog article. I created my first YouTube channel in 13 June, 2020. On this day I tribute to my back home college founder Spritual and Divine Soul Behan Devki Devi Jain. She is an inspiration to many girls she started school with 5 girls in Ludhiana, Punjab in 1918. I got opportunity to present her lifestory on her 116 birth anniversary. in front of college president, cheif guest and more than 2000 audience. Media was there and they record my video and posted on news channel. On that I decided to show people about her biography and inspirational stories to those who wants to do something impactful in life. From that day I got an idea to become content creator to create knowledgeable content for YouTube.

Thanks for reading my blog!

If I become something in my life I will always be thankful to you and mentioned you all in my next blogs. I know everybody have their goals and dreams and tough time as well. Right now I just want to say dont ever give up your dreams and aims even if it is harder and impossible becuase with your practice, consistency and faith in yourself can "Turns the Table".
